Deterministic Logic for the Calculator:
1. Selection Path: The user selects a Geographical Section (e.g., Section 1) and the Vessel LOA. The calculator pulls the base_rate from the tariff_a_matrix.sections nested rates.
2. Shifting Logic:
    ◦ For standard harbour shifts, it applies a 1.25 or 1.40 multiplier to the base_rate.
    ◦ For specific routes (like Merwedehaven), the calculator must pull the corresponding length-based price from Section 1 as its base, regardless of the ship's current location, and apply the specified 1.50x, 1.60x, or 1.75x multiplier.
3. Surcharge Stacking: Surcharges for Dead Ships (1.5x) or Holidays (1.25x) are applied to the calculated move rate.
4. Waiting Time: If duration > 30 mins, the calculator bills in half-hour increments starting from the first minute of the second half hour.
